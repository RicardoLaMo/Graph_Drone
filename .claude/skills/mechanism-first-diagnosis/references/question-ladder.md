# Mechanism Question Ladder

## Component Truth

- Did the claimed local signal move?
- Was it finite and reproducible?

## Circuit Coupling

- Did the surrounding policy or allocation logic change?
- Did it change in the intended direction?

## Outcome Translation

- Which metric family moved?
- Could calibration, thresholding, or latency explain the mismatch?

## Reproducibility

- Is the contract identical?
- Are seeds and versions controlled?
- Did any fallback or instability invalidate a naïve interpretation?

## Next Check

- What is the smallest falsifying check now?
